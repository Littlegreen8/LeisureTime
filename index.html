About the Project

Australia is a wealthy country which has a high GDP per capita and low rate of poverty. Australia also has very high minimum wages rate (A$18.93) across developed countries. To the employment, Australia has ranges of industries and job opportunities across the continent, the pay rates and employment circumstance are varied in different states.
